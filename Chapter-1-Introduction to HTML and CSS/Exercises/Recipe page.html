Lotus Biscoff Dessert

Creamy Lotus Biscoff dessert consists of layers of crispy biscuits, smooth cream, and dense Lotus spread.

Ingredients

  • A single packet of Lotus Biscoff biscuits.
  • One half cup of melted Lotus Biscoff spread.
  • An whole cup of whipped cream.
  • One half cup of cream cheese.
  • A quarter of a cup of refined sugar.
  • A single spoonful of vanilla extract.
  • Optional extra lotus crumbs for garnish.

Method

  1. In a bowl, beat the cream until soft peaks form, before placing aside.
  2. Stir the vanilla extract, cream cheese, and refinedÂ sugar in a separate bowl until fluffy.
  3. The whipped cream should be gently incorporated into the cream cheese mixture.
  4. Set the crumbled Lotus biscuits on the bottom of the platter or in serving glasses.
  5. Coat the biscuits with a layer of the cream mixture.
  6. Drizzle the cream layer with melted Lotus spread.
  7. If desired, repeat layers and sprinkle with biscuit crumbs and Lotus spread.
  8. Before eating, let it settle in the freezer for at least two hours.
